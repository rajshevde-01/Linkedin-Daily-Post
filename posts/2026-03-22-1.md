Ransomware pays the devil.

We're seeing a rise in MCP security risks that can't be patched away. 

1. Pay the ransom and risk funding future attacks.
2. Refuse to pay and attempt recovery from backups.
3. Try to negotiate with the attackers, potentially prolonging downtime.

I'd refuse to pay and attempt recovery. Backups are often incomplete and can't restore everything.
 SIEM evasions require Zero Trust.
